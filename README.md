# 🌍 Global Mental Health Insights Dashboard

## 🧠 Project Overview
This project analyzes global mental health data to uncover patterns in patient outcomes, treatment effectiveness, and key risk factors such as anxiety, stress levels, and lifestyle variables.

The goal of this analysis is to provide data-driven insights that can support better mental health interventions and decision-making.

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Global mental health dataset
- Approximate size: 2,500 records
- Key features include:
  - Country
  - Age Band
  - Gender
  - Work Status
  - Anxiety Level
  - Depression Score
  - Sleep Hours
  - Physical Activity
  - Treatment Type
  - Mental Health Outcome

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- **Microsoft Excel / Power Query** – Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard development  

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed
- Distribution of mental health outcomes across different demographics  
- Impact of physical activity on mental health  
- Treatment effectiveness (therapy, medication, both, or none)  
- Relationship between chronic illness and mental health outcomes  
- Analysis of anxiety levels across the patient population  
- Comparison of mental health outcomes by work status, age group, gender and country 

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
The interactive Power BI dashboard includes:

- **KPI Cards**
  - Total Patients
  - % Severe Stress Level
  - Treatment Success Rate
  - Average Anxiety Score
  - Average Depression Score  

- **Visual Insights**
  - Mental health outcomes by physical activity & sleep
  - Treatment effectiveness by outcome
  - Impact of chronic illness on outcomes
  - Mental health outcomes by selected category
  - Distribution of anxiety levels (donut chart)

- **Interactive Filters (Slicers)**
  - Age Band
  - Country
  - Gender
  - Work Status
  - Outcome

## 💡 Key Insights
- Higher levels of physical activity are associated with better mental health outcomes  
- Patients receiving treatment (especially therapy or combined treatment) show improved outcomes  
- A significant proportion of patients experience moderate to severe anxiety levels  
- Patients with chronic illnesses are more likely to experience poorer mental health outcomes, suggesting a strong link between physical and mental health conditions  
- Work status and age group influence mental health patterns across the population  

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This analysis highlights the importance of lifestyle factors, access to treatment, and demographic differences in shaping mental health outcomes. The insights generated can help inform targeted mental health strategies and interventions.
